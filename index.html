About me

I am a PhD candidate of Xidian University, Xi'an, China.

I received my BSc. Degree (2012) in Electronic and Information Engineering, School of Electronic Engineering, Xidian University. Then I began to pursue my doctor degree in VIPSL lab supervised by Prof. Xinbo Gao from Sep. 2012.

My research interests include pattern recognition, machine learning, computer vision, and applications to heterogeneous image transformation and heterogeneous face recognition.
